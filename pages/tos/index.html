Tomes of Solitude

This was my first entry in the Ludum Dare competition, where you have to make a game in 48 hours. It's a game about a wizard in search of power. You need to collect the 5 Tomes of Solitude. There are three bosses you have to fight in order to beat the game. You can find the game entry on the Ludum Dare website. The game finished 31st out of 717 entries in the overall category and 10th out of 717 in the fun category.
