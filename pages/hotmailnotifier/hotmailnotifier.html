Hotmail Notifier

Hotmail Notifier is a plugin for http://miranda-im.org that makes it possible to check your windows live hotmail inboxes. One or more contacts, one for each hotmail account, can be added to your contact list. These will then check your inbox every fifteen or so minutes. If an unread e-mail is present in your inbox you will be notified of this using popups and the contact in your contact list.

Notice (21-06-2009)

Previously Hotmail Notifier was hosted on the miranda-im addons page. But after Hotmail added pop access to their service the plugin became really obsolete, and with me not having used Miranda for over a long time I've decided to remove it from the addons page. The plugin will however remain available on this website.

Features

  • Multiple accounts
  • Popups
  • Updater support
  • Renaming of contact created in contact list
  • Customizing of icons
